# Click-by-Click — One Person, From Found to Hired

Same rough, no-tech tone as the walkthrough — but now literal. We follow **one nurse, "Sarah,"**
from the moment you find her to the day she starts the job. Every step is what you actually click.

Companion to `PRODUCT-WALKTHROUGH.md` (the big-picture story). This is the boots-on-the-ground
version of the flagship flow.

---

## Step 0 — Get in
1. Open the app.
2. Type your email + password → hit **Sign In** (or click the **Google** button).
3. You land on **Overview**. Good. You're in.

## Step 1 — Find Sarah (Discover)
1. Left menu → click **Discover**.
2. Pick a credential ("Nurse Practitioner") and a state ("Florida"). Hit **Search**.
3. A list of real providers shows up. Find Sarah in the list.
4. Click **Add to Sourcing** next to her name.
   → Sarah is now a **lead** (a "maybe," not hired yet).

## Step 2 — Chase her (Sourcing + Outreach)
1. Left menu → click **Sourcing**. Sarah's in your list.
2. Click her row → click **Log Outreach**.
3. Pick the channel (LinkedIn / Email), write nothing or a note, hit **Save**.
   → Her status ticks to **Outreach 1**. That's your 1st poke.
4. No reply in a few days? Log outreach again → **Outreach 2**.
5. She replies "yeah I'm interested!" → open her row → set status to **Responded — Hot**. 🔥

> **Shortcut when a reply comes in cold (Inbound Triage):** if Sarah messaged you *first* (a
> LinkedIn DM or email) and she's not in your list yet, go to **Inbound** → paste her message →
> hit **Triage**. The AI reads it, pulls out her details, and creates her as a **hot lead** already
> matched to a role — no manual typing. Then continue to Promote below.

## Step 3 — Pull her into the real line (Promote)
1. Still on Sarah's row → click **Promote**.
2. Fill the short form: which client, her credential, license state. Hit **Promote**.
   → Boom. Sarah leaves the maybe-pile and becomes a real **candidate** in the hiring line.
   The system jumps you straight to her new card.

## Step 4 — Load her resume (Parse Resume)
1. Left menu → click **Parse Resume**.
2. Pick the role type (Clinical). Drag her PDF resume onto the box (or click to browse).
3. Hit **Extract**. The AI reads it and fills in her details for you.
4. Eyeball it, fix anything wrong → attach it to Sarah.

## Step 5 — The hiring line (Pipeline)
1. Left menu → click **Pipeline**. You see the big board with columns (stages).
2. Sarah's card sits in an early column. To move her forward, **drag her card** to the next column.
   - If you try to skip a required step (like sending her out before her license is checked), the
     system **stops you** and tells you what's missing. That's on purpose.

## Step 6 — Grade her (Screening)
1. Click Sarah's card → the **full folder** opens. (Or use the **Screening** menu.)
2. Tick off her credentials, states, experience, pay expectation.
3. The system spits out a **score** and a verdict: **Advance / Conditional / Hold**.
4. Good score → she's ready to show a client.

## Step 7 — Check her license (Verify)
1. In Sarah's folder → **License** tab.
2. Click the state-board link to confirm her license is real + active.
3. Set her license status to **Active** → **Save**. Green light. ✅

## Step 8 — Send her to the clinic (Submit to Client)
1. Back on the **Pipeline** board → drag Sarah's card to **Submitted to Client**.
   → Now the clinic can see her (through the **Client Portal** window you share with them).
2. Want to email the clinic about her? Left menu → **Templates** → pick "Candidate Presentation"
   → it auto-fills Sarah's info → hit **Open in Gmail** → send.

## Step 9 — Interview → Offer → Hired
1. Clinic wants to talk to her → drag her card to **Client Interview**.
2. They like her, make an offer → drag to **Offer / Negotiation**.
3. She says yes → drag to **Offer Accepted**.
4. First day on the job → drag to **Started (Day 1)**. 🎉
   → The system quietly hands her off to the "day-one" side. Sarah is officially placed.

## Step 10 — Log your win & get out
1. Left menu → **Daily Log** → punch in what you did today (sourced, messaged, submitted).
2. Done for the day? Top-right → **Sign Out**. Doors lock. See you tomorrow.

---

## The whole thing in one breath
> **Discover** Sarah → **Sourcing** to chase her → **Promote** her in → **Parse** her resume →
> drag her across the **Pipeline** → **Screen** + **Verify** → **Submit** to the clinic →
> **Interview → Offer → Started** → **Log** it → **Sign out.**

That's one human, start to finish. Every other feature in the app is just a helper hanging off
one of these clicks.
